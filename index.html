Time line of Charles Darwin

  • 1809 - He was born in Shrewsbury.
  • 1825 - He enters the University of Edinburgh to study medicine.
  • 1828 - Begins a theology degree at Cambridge.
  • 1831-1836 - Travel with the Beagle as a naturalist for a voyage of exploration. among them, the galapagos islands
  • 1842 - First draft of the theory of evolution.
  • 1859 - Publishes The Origin of Species.
  • 1862 - Publishes On the intervention of insects in the fertilization of orchids.
  • 1871 - Publishes The Descent of Man and Selection in Realtion to Sex.
  • 1881 - Publishes On the influence of earthworms in the formation of topsoil.
  • 1882 - Charles Darwin dies.
